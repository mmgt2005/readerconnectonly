🔵 Bluetooth Connection Tips

  • Make sure your M2 reader is turned on and in range
  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on this device
  • Try moving closer to the reader (within 30 feet)
  • If connection fails, restart your reader and try again

📍 Manual Location Permission Setup

  1. Click the location/lock icon in your browser's address bar
  2. Set "Location" to "Allow" or "Ask"
  3. Refresh this page and try connecting again

Note: Location permission is required by browsers for Bluetooth device discovery.
